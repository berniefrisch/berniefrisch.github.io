Welcome to my site! My name is Bernie Frisch, and I am the creator of Demon Lands, my upcoming graphic novel series!
I draw inspiration for my works from Bryan Lee O'Malley (best known as the creator of the "<a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Scott_Pilgrim">Scott Pilgrim"</a> and "<a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seconds_(comics)">Seconds</a>") and Akira Toriyama (best known as the creator of "<a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dragon_Ball">Dragon Ball</a>")!
I'm Jewish, autistic, and nonbinary.
Welp, that's all!
