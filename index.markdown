I draw inspiration for my works from Bryan Lee O'Malley (best known as the creator of the "<a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Scott_Pilgrim">Scott Pilgrim</a>" series and the "<a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seconds_(comics)">Seconds</a>" comic book) and Akira Toriyama (best known as the creator of "<a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dragon_Ball">Dragon Ball</a>")!
I'm Jewish, autistic, and nonbinary.
